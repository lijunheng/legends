Stroop Test

In this experiment, we are interested in the effect of reward on conflict resolution. You will be presented with a word (either 'BLUE' or 'PINK') in either blue or pink text color. Your task to indicate the color of the text ('p' key if the text is pink, 'b' key if the text is blue), not the color indicated by the text.

You will also get points depending on whether the trial is high reward (star) or low reward (circle). The reward is cued before each trial. The experiment will last about 1 minute.
